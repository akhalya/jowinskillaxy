Our Programs

We envision a future where every child has the opportunity to unlock their full potential through quality education and personalized learning experiences.

Abacus Training

Master mental arithmetic, improve concentration, and enhance memory skills through our Abacus training program. Abacus training is proven to boost math proficiency and cognitive abilities in children.

Learn more
Phonetic Reading English

Our Phonetic Reading English program is designed to help children develop strong reading and comprehension skills through a phonetic-based approach. Phonics is an essential foundation for literacy, teaching students to recognize and understand the sounds that letters make, which is fundamental to reading and spelling.

Learn more
Mathematics (1-12th Grade)

Our Mathematics program offers comprehensive instruction tailored to students from first grade through twelfth grade, providing a structured and engaging learning experience that builds a strong foundation in mathematical concepts and problem-solving skills.

Learn more
Special Classes

Our Special Classes are suitable for students of all ages and skill levels who are interested in exploring their creativity, expanding their horizons, and gaining practical skills in the arts and technology.
